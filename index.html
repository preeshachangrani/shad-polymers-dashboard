BEARISH PVC

PVC June 2026: Range-Bound at Low Levels, Demand Sluggish

The PVC market extended weak range-bound trading from May. Ethylene-based units restarted in June, increasing supply pressure as demand remains sluggish. Real estate applications — over 60% of PVC consumption — continue to weigh. Operating rates dropped sharply in May, marking the strongest supply contraction in recent years. Both calcium carbide-based and ethylene-based producers are in loss-making territory; coal and calcium carbide costs provide a floor. Market expected to remain weak through June.
