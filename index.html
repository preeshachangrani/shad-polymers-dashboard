Polymer & Terms Reference

PP (Polypropylene)
Raffia, Homo, Copo and Random grades sourced mainly from China (CHN Energy, Jinneng/Baofeng, Oriental Energy). Used for woven bags, packaging film, injection molding. Active price sheet PP range: $1,430–$1,550/MT (PP + PPCP grades); uploaded SHAD TOPWAY sheet shows a wider $1,360–$1,560/MT CFR Apapa across more grades.
PE (Polyethylene)
LDPE, LLDPE, MLLDPE, HDPE (Injection/Blow/Film/Pipe Natural) cover the bulk of quoted grades. Used in film, blow-molded containers, pipe. Active price sheet PE range: $1,420–$1,915/MT; uploaded SHAD TOPWAY sheet shows $1,250–$1,705/MT CFR Apapa across more grades.
PVC (Polyvinyl Chloride)
SG-5 and related suspension grades, used for pipe, profiles, cable insulation. Active price sheet currently has only 1 priced PVC quote ($900/MT); the uploaded SHAD TOPWAY sheet shows 3 grades at $875–$975/MT CFR Apapa.
Payment Terms Glossary
DA-60/DA-90: Documents against Acceptance, payment due 60/90 days after shipment/acceptance. CAD: Cash Against Documents, paid on presentation of shipping documents. LC: Letter of Credit. PFI: Pro-Forma Invoice, the buyer-facing order confirmation that anchors each pipeline order from PFI date through final payment.
