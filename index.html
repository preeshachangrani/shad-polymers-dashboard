Polymer & Terms Reference

PP (Polypropylene)
Raffia, Homo, Copo and Random grades sourced mainly from China (CHN Energy, Jinneng/Baofeng, Oriental Energy) plus Saudi (Sabic, Sumitomo) and Taiwan (Formosa). Used for woven bags, packaging film, injection molding. Nigeria active price sheet PP range: $1,430–$1,550/MT; SHAD TOPWAY CFR Apapa sheet (22-Jun) spans $1,345–$1,560/MT across 19 grades.
PE (Polyethylene)
LDPE, LLDPE, MLLDPE, HDPE (Injection/Blow/Film/Pipe Natural) cover the bulk of quoted grades across Nigeria, Sri Lanka, Brazil and Cotonou. Used in film, blow-molded containers, pipe. Global spot reference (24 Jun): HDPE ~$1,270/MT, LLDPE ~$1,250/MT, both down $25 day-on-day on easing Middle East supply risk.
PVC (Polyvinyl Chloride)
SG-5 and DG-series suspension grades, used for pipe, profiles, cable insulation. Nigeria active price sheet has 1 priced quote ($900/MT); SHAD TOPWAY sheet shows 3 grades at $875–$995/MT CFR Apapa. China PVC spot is correcting modestly (-8.2% m/m) after a long uptrend, with carbide-route supply keeping a low ceiling on prices.
PET
Bottle-grade PET (CR8816, CZ302) is the main quoted family, sourced via CRC and ELAINE for the Nigeria/Sri Lanka book. STARPOINT is the primary customer for Nigeria PET quotes seen in this run; GP POLYMER quotes PET in Sri Lanka but without a matched offer price yet.
