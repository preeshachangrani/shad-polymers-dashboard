Polymer & Terms Reference

PP (Polypropylene)
Raffia, Homo, Copo and Random grades sourced mainly from China (CHN Energy, Jinneng/Baofeng, Oriental Energy) plus Saudi (Sabic, Sumitomo) and Taiwan (Formosa). Used for woven bags, packaging film, injection molding. May 2026 regional benchmarks: ~$1.43/kg NE Asia, ~$1.31/kg India, ~$2.53/kg Europe — outlook weak-to-stable across all regions.
PE (Polyethylene)
LDPE, LLDPE, MLLDPE, HDPE (Injection/Blow/Film/Pipe Natural) cover the bulk of quoted grades across Nigeria, Sri Lanka, Brazil and Cotonou. Used in film, blow-molded containers, pipe. China PE futures sit at 6,875 CNY/T (29 Jun), down 13.4% over the past month and 5.78% y/y; US HDPE reached $1,194/MT in June on firm packaging/construction demand, while Southeast Asian converters have stopped panic-buying as regional supply lengthens.
PVC (Polyvinyl Chloride)
SG-5 and DG-series suspension grades, used for pipe, profiles, cable insulation. Nigeria active price sheet has 1 priced quote ($900/MT); SHAD TOPWAY sheet shows 3 grades at $875–$995/MT CFR Apapa. China PVC futures fell 2.06% to 4,377 CNY/T (29 Jun), staying range-bound at low levels as real-estate-linked demand lags; India PVC ~$893/MT (construction-led upside) vs Brazil ~$472/MT in June.
PET
Bottle-grade PET (CR8816, CZ302) is the main quoted family, sourced via CRC and ELAINE for the Nigeria/Sri Lanka book. STARPOINT is the primary customer for Nigeria PET quotes seen in this run; GP POLYMER quotes PET in Sri Lanka but without a matched offer price yet.
